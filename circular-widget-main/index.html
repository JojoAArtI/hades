// HISTORICAL RECORD 01

THE ESCAPE

Deep beneath the mortal realm lies the domain of Hades. Zagreus, the rebellious Prince of the Underworld, is determined to fight his way through the shifting chambers of his father's house to discover the secrets of his past.

// HISTORICAL RECORD 02

Zagreus lived under the belief that Nyx, the Goddess of Night, was his mother. But upon discovering hidden letters in his father's drawer, he learned the truth: his real mother was Persephone, the former Queen of the Underworld, who had fled to the mortal surface. This discovery ignited his resolve to break out.

// HISTORICAL RECORD 03

The Olympians—Zeus, Poseidon, Athena, Ares, Artemis, Aphrodite, Hermes, Dionysus, and Demeter—hear his plea. Believing he is fighting his way to join them on Olympus, they send their divine Boons. Yet Zagreus also relies on the dark forces of the Underworld, using the Mirror of Night to enhance his capabilities.

// HISTORICAL RECORD 04

At the surface threshold, Zagreus defeats his father, Hades. He finally steps into the warm sunlight of Greece and meets Persephone. But the Fates are cruel—he is bound to the Underworld by his birth. If he stays on the surface too long, his life force fades. He dies, returning to the Pool of Styx, only to start his journey again.
