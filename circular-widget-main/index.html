THE STORY

I.

THE REBELLIOUS PRINCE

Zagreus, the rebellious prince of the Underworld, seeks to escape the domain of his stifling father, Hades, to find his long-lost mother, Persephone, in the mortal world.

II.

OLYMPIAN AID

Recognizing his struggle, the mighty Olympians lend their strength, offering powerful Boons to assist Zagreus on his hazardous journey through the depths of hell.

III.

DEATH AND REBIRTH

Each failed escape attempt only returns Zagreus to the House of Hades, where he grows stronger, learns more secrets, and prepares to try again. Death is but a temporary setback.
